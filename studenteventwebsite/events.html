Our Events

🎭 Cultural Program

The Cultural Program celebrates the diverse talents and traditions of students through music, dance, drama, singing, and other performances. It provides a platform for self-expression, creativity, and cultural appreciation while bringing the college community together.

Cultural Program Schedule

Time Activity Description
10:00 AM - 10:30 AM Opening Performance Welcome dance by students.
10:30 AM - 11:30 AM Singing Competition Solo and group singing performances.
11:30 AM - 12:30 PM Dance Competition Traditional and modern dance performances.
1:30 PM - 2:30 PM Drama Performance Stage plays and theatrical acts.
2:30 PM - 3:30 PM Talent Show Students showcase unique talents.
3:30 PM - 4:00 PM Award Ceremony Distribution of prizes and certificates.

🏆 Sports Week

Sports Week promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship through various athletic competitions and games. Students participate in football, basketball, athletics, and other sporting events, fostering healthy competition and an active lifestyle.

Sports Week Schedule

Time Activity Description
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM Opening Ceremony Introduction and sports oath.
9:00 AM - 11:00 AM Football Match Inter-department football competition.
11:00 AM - 12:30 PM Basketball Tournament Team-based basketball matches.
1:30 PM - 2:30 PM 100m & 200m Race Track and field sprint events.
2:30 PM - 3:30 PM Tug of War Team strength and coordination event.
3:30 PM - 4:30 PM Volleyball Match Friendly competition between teams.
4:30 PM - 5:00 PM Prize Distribution Winners receive medals, trophies, and certificates.

Why Participate?

  • Develop new skills and talents
  • Build confidence and teamwork
  • Meet and connect with other students
  • Showcase your abilities
  • Win prizes and certificates
  • Create memorable college experiences
